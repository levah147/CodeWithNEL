About CodeWithNEL

Learn more about our mission to empower developers through multi-platform educational content.

Our Mission

At CodeWithNEL, we believe that programming education should be accessible, engaging, and available across multiple platforms. Our mission is to empower developers of all skill levels by providing high-quality tutorials that are both informative and entertaining.

Who We Are

CodeWithNEL was founded by NEL, a passionate software developer and educator with over 10 years of experience in the industry. What started as a small YouTube channel has grown into a multi-platform educational hub reaching hundreds of thousands of developers worldwide.

Our Approach

We take a unique approach to programming education by:

  • Meeting learners where they are - Whether you prefer long-form YouTube tutorials, quick TikTok tips, or professional LinkedIn content, we've got you covered.
  • Focusing on practical skills - Our tutorials emphasize real-world applications and projects that you can add to your portfolio.
  • Building a supportive community - Learning to code is better with friends. We foster a welcoming environment for developers to connect and grow together.

Our Platforms

You can find CodeWithNEL content on:

  • YouTube - In-depth tutorials and project walkthroughs
  • TikTok - Quick tips, tricks, and coding challenges
  • Facebook - Community discussions and live coding sessions
  • LinkedIn - Professional development content and industry insights
